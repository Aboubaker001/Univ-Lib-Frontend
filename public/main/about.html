Our History & Mission

Established in 1995 alongside El Oued University, UniLibrary has grown into a cornerstone of academic excellence in southeastern Algeria. With over 250,000 resources, including books, journals, and digital archives, we support students, faculty, and researchers across disciplines.

Our mission is to foster a culture of learning and innovation by providing access to world-class resources, cutting-edge technology, and collaborative spaces. We are committed to empowering the university community and beyond.
