Master Your Library Search

Learn how to find books, journals, and digital resources quickly and effectively.

Search Tips

Search by Title

Enter the full or partial book title to find exact matches or related resources.

"Introduction to Quantum Computing"

Search by Author

Use author names to locate books by specific writers or editors.

"Jane Smith"

Search by ISBN

Enter the ISBN for precise results, especially for specific editions.

"978-3-16-148410-0"

Search by Keywords

Use relevant keywords to discover books on specific topics.

"quantum computing, physics"

Filter by Status

Check if a book is available, checked out, or reserved before reserving.

Filter: AVAILABLE

Advanced Search

Combine fields like title, author, and language for precise results.

"title:Quantum author:Smith lang:English"

How to Reserve a Book

Step 1: Search & Select

Find your book using the search bar and check its availability.

Step 2: Reserve

Click "Reserve" if the book is available. You can reserve up to 3 books.

Step 3: Confirmation

Wait for librarian approval. You'll be notified via email.

Step 4: Pickup

Visit the library to pick up your book before the reservation expires.
